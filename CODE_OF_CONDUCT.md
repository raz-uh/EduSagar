# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants to uphold these principles and help us foster a respectful and inclusive environment.

## Expected Behavior

All contributors and community members are expected to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat others with respect and dignity
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and criticism
- **Be professional** - Maintain professionalism in all interactions
- **Be collaborative** - Work together toward common goals

## Unacceptable Behavior

We do not tolerate:

- **Harassment** - Unwelcome comments about personal characteristics
- **Discrimination** - Treatment based on age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity, or sexual orientation
- **Abusive language** - Offensive or derogatory language
- **Intimidation** - Threatening or intimidating others
- **Unwelcome attention** - Sexual harassment or unwanted romantic attention
- **Trolling** - Disrupting discussions with provocative or off-topic comments
- **Doxxing** - Publishing private information without consent
- **Spam** - Repeatedly promoting products or services

## Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report privately** - Email support@edusagar.dev with details
2. **Include context** - Describe what happened and who was involved
3. **Provide evidence** - Include screenshots or links if possible
4. **Be specific** - Help us understand the violation

All reports will be reviewed confidentially and promptly.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Warning or request for apology
- Temporary suspension from community
- Permanent ban from project and community

The severity of the response will depend on the nature and frequency of violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All repositories and projects
- Issue tracker and pull requests
- Community discussions and forums
- Conference talks and presentations
- Social media channels
- Community events

## Questions?

Contact us at support@edusagar.dev
